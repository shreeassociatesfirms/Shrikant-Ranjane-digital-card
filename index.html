03 — Services

About Our Services

Shree Associates is a premier legal and property compliance consultancy with over two decades of proven excellence. We specialise in guiding cooperative societies, property developers, and individuals through complex legal frameworks with clarity, transparency, and efficiency — delivering customised solutions tailored to each client's unique requirements.

Society Registration & Conveyance

  • Society Registration — Complete guidance and documentation support for registration of Housing, Credit & Seva societies, Trusts, and Mandals, ensuring smooth approval as per government regulations.
  • Conveyance Deed & Deemed Conveyance — Professional assistance transferring property ownership rights from builder to society, including complete documentation and legal compliance.

CIDCO Transfer & Stamp Duty

Expert support for CIDCO flat, shop & plot transfer, lease matters and mortgage clearance — plus complete Stamp Duty & Registration assistance for lease deeds, leave & license, and flat/shop registration.

Compliance & Legal Advisory

  • Accounting, Audit & Administration — Society accounts management, statutory audit, monthly billing, and financial reporting.
  • Redevelopment Advisory — Legal guidance for redevelopment projects, developer agreements, and PMC appointment.
  • Legal Services — Civil, criminal, consumer complaints & co-operative society disputes.

4 Case Studies

Illustrative examples of the legal and property challenges we've resolved for clients.

01

Stalled Housing Society Registration Resolved

A newly formed housing society had its registration stuck for over a year due to incomplete documentation. We reorganised the paperwork, liaised directly with the registrar's office, and secured approval within 60 days.

02

Deemed Conveyance After Builder Delay

A society had waited years for its builder to execute the conveyance deed. We filed for Deemed Conveyance, prepared the complete legal case, and secured ownership transfer without further builder cooperation.

03

CIDCO Flat Transfer & Mortgage Clearance

A client faced a complex CIDCO lease and mortgage clearance issue blocking their flat transfer. We managed the complete documentation and coordination, resulting in a clean transfer within the expected timeline.

04

Redevelopment Advisory for Ageing Society

An ageing society needed guidance on redevelopment but lacked legal clarity on developer agreements. We advised on PMC appointment and drafted a transparent agreement protecting member interests.
